Formwork

Precast
Formwork
Concrete Batching Plant
Construction, Equipment, Logistics, and Services

Formwork Systems

Single-Sided Formwork

Used for standard wall heights

Wall Formwork

Used for casting walls, support walls pillars, earth retaining walls, and other vertical type castings

Column Formwork

Allows quick assembly and erection on site

Guided Climbing System

Usually used for walls in high-rise construction

Slab Formwork

Allows concrete to be poured and suspended above the ground; supports the weight of the concrete while it sets.

Guided Climbing Screen

Promotes optimum work safety especially on slabs or slab edges on high-rise constructions.

Automatic Climbing System

A crane-independent climbing system, operated using hydraulic drive; used for tall concrete structures.
